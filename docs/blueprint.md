# **App Name**: Elaichi Essence

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Full-width hero section with background image, headline, subheading, and primary/secondary call-to-action buttons.
- About Section: Overview of Cafe Elaichi, highlighting its unique selling points with icons and scroll animations.
- Menu Highlights: Showcase signature drinks, snacks, and desserts with images, descriptions, prices, and a 'Explore Full Menu' CTA.
- Gallery / Instagram Feed: A visual gallery showcasing café interiors, food, and customer experiences, potentially integrated with Instagram.
- Testimonials Carousel: Display rotating customer reviews and ratings.
- Location & Contact: Display map, contact info, opening hours, and a contact form.
- Table Reservation: Simple reservation form with date, time, and guest count.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A soft, natural green (#8FBC8F) to evoke a sense of freshness and connection to nature.
- Background color: A light, desaturated green (#F0FFF0) to create a calming and airy backdrop. 
- Accent color: A slightly warmer green (#6B8E23), to suggest richness and spice.
- Body text: 'PT Sans', a modern, readable sans-serif font, well-suited for both headers and blocks of text
- Use minimalist, line-based icons in the primary green color (#8FBC8F) to complement the café theme.
- Use a clean, grid-based layout to ensure responsiveness and easy navigation on all devices.
- Incorporate subtle animations, such as fading and sliding effects, to enhance user experience without being distracting.
